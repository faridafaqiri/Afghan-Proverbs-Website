"با صبر، ترش شیرین می‌شود"

"With patience, the sour turns sweet."

This proverb teaches that difficult situations improve with patience and perseverance.

"یک دست صدا ندارد"

"One hand cannot clap alone."

Meaning cooperation is needed to accomplish things.

About Afghan Proverbs

Afghan proverbs—known as "مثل‌ها" (Matal-ha) in Dari and Pashto—are short, poetic expressions deeply rooted in Afghan culture. Passed down through generations, they reflect the values, humor, struggles, and moral lessons of the Afghan people. These proverbs are a fundamental part of daily conversation, storytelling, and oral traditions across Afghanistan's diverse ethnic groups.
